Puppy and Dog Socialisation

Some owners get anxious about their dogs/puppies interacting with other dogs as they may feel their dogs are over excited/ too boisterous, nervous or act aggressively. Therefore they avoid other dogs when out walking or pull their dog away when another dog approaches. Allowing your dog to interact with others is very important for their mental and physical well-being.

We can help you and your dog gain confidence by careful introductions with the right dogs in a controlled environment.
